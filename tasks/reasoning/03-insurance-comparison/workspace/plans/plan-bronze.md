# Bronze Plan — "EssentialCare"
Monthly premium (family of 4): $620
Annual deductible: $7,500 family
Out-of-pocket max: $14,000 family
Network: BlueNet (PCP, pediatrics included); specialist coverage requires referral
Copay structure:
  - PCP visit: $30
  - Specialist visit: $80 (after deductible)
  - ER visit: $500 (waived if admitted)
Prescription:
  - Generic: $15 / month
  - Preferred brand: $50 / month
  - Specialty (e.g. insulin): $120 / month after $500 drug deductible
Chronic disease management: NOT included as a separate program
Pediatric asthma: covered as standard specialist visits, after deductible

# Silver Plan — "BalancedCare"
Monthly premium (family of 4): $780
Annual deductible: $4,000 family
Out-of-pocket max: $9,500 family
Network: BlueNet (full)
Copay structure:
  - PCP visit: $25
  - Specialist visit: $50 (no referral needed)
  - ER visit: $350 (waived if admitted)
Prescription:
  - Generic: $10 / month
  - Preferred brand: $35 / month
  - Specialty (e.g. insulin): $60 / month, no separate drug deductible
Chronic disease management: Included — Diabetes Care Program with quarterly nurse check-ins, free glucose meter & test strips
Pediatric asthma: included in chronic care program (free spacer, free inhaler refills up to 4/year)
